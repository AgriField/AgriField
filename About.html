About Us

the use of websites in agriculture has allowed the industry to become more connected, efficient, and informative. As a result, websites have become an essential resource for those working in the agriculture industry. The art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops, and raising livestock are collectively referred to as agriculture. It involves preparing plants for human consumption and providing them to markets. Agriculture is the primary method of food production in the globe. To assist new or beginning farmers in understanding agricultural terminology, the Beginning Agriculture was created as a guide. It is intended to serve as a reference for understanding agricultural terminology for new or beginning farmers.
